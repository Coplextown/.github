## 🏙️ About

CoplexTown is an internet-native city built for developers.

Instead of being a single project, CoplexTown is a collection of communities, tools, products, experiments, and knowledge bases designed to help software creators thrive.

Whether you're shipping production software, experimenting with new ideas, or learning something completely new, there's a place for you here.

---

## 🏛️ Districts

### 🌳 CoplexPark
A developer community.

Meet people, share projects, discuss ideas, and grow together.

### 🧪 CoplexLab
An experimental laboratory.

Home to prototypes, research, side projects, and technical explorations.

### 📰 CoplexTimes
